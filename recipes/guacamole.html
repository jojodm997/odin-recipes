Guacamole

Simply Guacamole

This is so easy and so good, it's always the hit of the party and it's gone before anything else on the table

Ingredients

  • 5 avocados - peeled,pitted, and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup minced fresh cilantro
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Directions

    Step 1

  1. Stir together the avocado and lemon juice in a serving bowl; add the green onion and cilantro; mix well. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately or store covered in refrigator with avocado pits in the bowl to keep from bowning
